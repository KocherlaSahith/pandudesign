About SOPA

India's Soybean Supply Chain — Made Transparent

The Soybean Processors Association platform enables processors to securely access verified farmer land parcel data, ensuring every soybean consignment is traceable from farm to factory — in full compliance with EUDR and domestic regulations.

  • Secure access to farmer data via Aadhaar & Agristack IDs
  • End-to-end traceability of soybean sourcing
  • EUDR-compliant audit logs stored immutably
  • Prepaid, payment-gated request submissions
  • Automated approval & refund workflows

Process Flow

How It Works

A simple, secure, six-step workflow from processor registration to verified data delivery.

1
Register & KYC
Processors register with company details, contact info, and KYC documents. Account is created upon successful verification.
2
Submit Data Request
Provide consignment details, farmer names, Aadhaar numbers, and Agristack IDs. The system validates all inputs.
3
Complete Payment
Pay via UPI, Net Banking, or Wallet. Payment is mandatory before request submission. A unique Request ID is generated.
4
SOPA Admin Review
SOPA Admin validates farmer details and payment status, then approves or rejects the request with full decision logs.
5
Agristack Data Fetch
Upon approval, the system fetches verified land parcel data from Agristack and securely delivers it to the processor.
6
Audit & Compliance
All request details, responses, and timestamps are stored immutably for EUDR traceability and regulatory audits.

Two Powerful Portals

Designed for Every Stakeholder

Processor Portal
For Soybean Processors

A dedicated portal for processors to register, submit farmer data requests, manage payments, and track approvals — all in one secure interface.

  • Company registration & OTP-based login
  • Submit & track land parcel requests
  • Integrated UPI / Net Banking payments
  • View approved data & download documents
  • Real-time status notifications (Email/SMS)
Admin Portal
For SOPA Administrators

Full control for SOPA admins to review incoming requests, validate farmer data, manage approvals, trigger Agristack fetches, and handle refunds.

  • Dashboard with all processor requests
  • Validate Aadhaar & payment details
  • Approve or reject with decision logs
  • Trigger Agristack land parcel fetch
  • Manage refunds & audit exports
